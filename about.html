FAQS

Frequently Ask Question

WHow do I book a trip through your website?

You can use our website to search for flights, hotels, and rental cars. Once you have found what you are looking for, simply follow the prompts to book your trip.

Can I book a trip for someone else?

Yes, you can book a trip for someone else. When you enter the traveler information, just make sure to enter their name and contact information.

How do I cancel or change my reservation?

To cancel or change your reservation, you will need to contact the airline, hotel, or car rental company directly. Their contact information can be found in your confirmation email.

What is your refund policy?

Refund policies vary by airline, hotel, and car rental company. Please refer to their individual policies for more information.

Can I make changes to my itinerary after booking?

Yes, you can make changes to your itinerary after booking. However, please note that there may be fees associated with making changes.

How do I find the best deals on flights and hotels?

Use our search tool to compare prices from different airlines and hotels. You can also sign up for our email newsletter to receive exclusive deals and promotions.
